Who We Are

About DSSQ

Our Mission

The Data Science Society Quest (DSSQ) is a student-driven community at Quaid-e-Awam University of Engineering, Science and Technology, Nawabshah. We uncover the intricacies of data analysis, machine learning, and artificial intelligence — bringing together individuals eager to explore the vast landscape of data science.

Our Vision

We foster collaboration and knowledge exchange through hands-on workshops, insightful seminars, and engaging discussions led by experts in the field. Whether you're a seasoned data professional or a curious beginner, DSSQ promises an enriching experience that broadens your understanding of the ever-evolving world of data.
